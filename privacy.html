1. Who We Are

This Privacy Policy applies to lunallena.co and nextfullmoon.co, educational websites providing lunar calendar information, full moon dates, eclipse data, and mindfulness content. Both sites are operated by the same team and governed by this policy.

2. Information We Collect

We do not directly collect any personal information from visitors. We do not require registration, login, or any form to use our sites.

However, the following third-party services may collect data when you visit:

  • Google Analytics — collects anonymized usage statistics (pages visited, session duration, device type, approximate location). You can opt out via Google's opt-out tool.
  • Google AdSense — serves advertisements and may use cookies to show relevant ads based on your browsing history.
  • Vercel (our hosting provider) — may log access requests including IP addresses for infrastructure security purposes.

3. Cookies

Our sites do not set first-party cookies. Third-party services listed above (Google Analytics, Google AdSense) may set their own cookies. You can manage or disable cookies through your browser settings.

Google's advertising cookies are used to personalize ads. You can review and manage Google's ad settings at adssettings.google.com.

4. How Information Is Used

The third-party services on our sites use collected data to:

  • Analyze site traffic and improve content (Google Analytics)
  • Serve relevant advertisements (Google AdSense)
  • Maintain infrastructure security and performance (Vercel)

We do not sell, trade, or share any visitor data with additional third parties beyond the services listed above.

5. Advertising

These sites participate in the Google AdSense program. Google may use cookies and similar technologies to serve ads based on your prior visits to our site or other websites. Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads based on your visit to our sites and/or other sites on the Internet.

You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings or aboutads.info.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights under applicable privacy laws (including GDPR for EU residents and CCPA for California residents), including:

  • The right to know what data is collected about you
  • The right to request deletion of your data
  • The right to opt out of data sale (we do not sell data)
  • The right to non-discrimination for exercising your privacy rights

Since we do not directly collect personal data, most of these rights apply to the third-party services above. Please refer to Google's Privacy Policy for how to exercise your rights with Google.

7. Data Retention

We do not store any personal data on our own servers. Data retention policies of third-party services (Google Analytics, Google AdSense, Vercel) are governed by those services' own privacy policies.

8. Children's Privacy

Our sites are not directed at children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect any information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through our site, please contact us.
