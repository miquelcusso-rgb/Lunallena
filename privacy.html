Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Data We Collect

Lunallena / Next Full Moon does not collect personally identifiable data. There are no user accounts, no registration forms, and we do not store any information about you on any server.

Cookies & Local Storage

We use sessionStorage to remember whether you have already seen the PWA install banner during the current session. This information disappears when you close your browser and never leaves your device.

Google AdSense (described below) may use advertising cookies. These cookies are only set if you accept the consent notice shown on the site.

Google AdSense

We display ads served by Google AdSense. Google may use first-party and third-party cookies to personalise the ads shown based on your previous visits to this or other websites. You can review Google's privacy policy at policies.google.com/privacy and manage your ad preferences at google.com/settings/ads.

Google Fonts

We load typefaces from Google Fonts. Google may log the font request (IP address, user agent, referring URL). See the Google Fonts privacy FAQ.

Lunar Calculations

All astronomical calculations are performed locally in your browser. Your location is never sent to any server.

NASA SVS

Moon images are loaded directly from NASA servers (svs.gsfc.nasa.gov). NASA may log the image request. Images are in the public domain (US government work).

Third Parties

We do not share data with any third party beyond those listed above (Google AdSense, Google Fonts, NASA SVS).

Your Rights (GDPR)

If you are a resident of the European Union, you have the right of access, rectification, erasure, and data portability, as well as the right to object to processing. Since we do not collect personal data, these rights are not applicable in practice. For any enquiries, contact hello@nextfullmoon.co.

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority. In Spain: Agencia Española de Protección de Datos (aepd.es).
